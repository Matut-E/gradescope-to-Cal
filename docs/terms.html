Description of Service

Gradescope to Cal is a browser extension that automatically extracts assignment information from Gradescope.com, creates corresponding events in your Google Calendar, and displays your grades at a glance. The service operates entirely within your browser using a zero-server architecture.

You are responsible for:

  • Using the extension in compliance with your institution's policies
  • Ensuring you have proper access to Gradescope and Google Calendar
  • Verifying assignment due dates and details independently
  • Verifying grades on your school's official grading platform - grades displayed in the extension are for convenience only
