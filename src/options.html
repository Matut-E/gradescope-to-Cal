                ‚ùì Why doesn't my calculated grade match Gradescope?
            

            

                

                    Common reasons for differences:
                


                
    
                    
  1. Curves or adjustments - Professors often curve final grades after seeing full class distribution
    2. 
                    
  2. Extra credit outside Gradescope - Participation points, iClicker, attendance, etc.
    3. 
                    
  3. Rounding differences - We round at each step; professors might round only at the end
    4. 
                    
  4. Misconfigured weights - Double-check your configured weights match the actual syllabus
    5. 
                    
  5. Assignment categorization errors - Review assignments with low confidence in the review section
    6. 
                    
  6. Drop policy differences - Make sure "drop lowest N" settings match course policy
    7. 
                    
  7. Late penalties - Gradescope may apply penalties we don't account for
    8. 
                


                

                    This is normal!
                    

                        The calculator is a planning tool to help you track progress and set goals.
                        Small differences (1-3%) from official grades are expected and don't indicate a problem.
                    

                


                

                    Still confused? Ask your GSI or professor how they calculate the final grade.
                    You can then adjust your configuration to match their exact formula.
